Pizza Margherita!

Margherita

Ingredients

Dough
Pizza sauce
Mozarella cheese(thinly sliced)
Extra olive oil
Basil

Directions

1.Preheat oven as hot as it can be
2.Using your hands shape the dough's center to be thin and the crust of the dough to be extra thick
3.Spread sauce over the dough and place the cheese over
4.Put the pizza into the pre-heated oven and set it to 220-225 degree celsius
5.Take pizza out of the oven and top with basil and extra olive oil
6.Enjoy!
