Dough!

Dough

Ingredients


All purpose flour
Granulated Sugar
Olive oil
Salt
Water
Yeast

Directions

1.Mix yeast with sugar and warm water to let it proof for a few minutes
2.After proofing mix olive oil to the mixture and stir
3.Add flour and salt then combine, adding flour to control stickiness 4.After getting to a slightly sticky ball, move to a floured surface and knead 5.(OPTIONAL)You can let the dough rise before using it
