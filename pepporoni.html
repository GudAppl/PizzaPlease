Pepperonni!

Pepporoni

Ingredients

Dough
Pizza sauce
Pepperoni
Cheese

Directions

1.Preheat oven
2.Roll dough into a thin circle
3.Spread sauce over the dough and spread cheese over
4.Top with Pepperoni
5.Put the pizza into the pre-heated oven and set it to 220-225 degree celsius
6.Take pizza out of the oven and enjoy
