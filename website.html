About PizzaPlease

Reason

Personally I made this website since
Pizza has always had a special place in my heart.
I loved it ever since I was young, be it
restaurant or home made. Those nights where my parents
brought home were enjoyable to me. So you can
consider this as an outlet to my appreciation for pizza!

Purposes

This website is to serve as an online cookbook of sorts
,However I decided to focus solely on one of my favourite
types of dish "Pizza." I want to help people learn how to
cook for themselves. I personally know the joy of learning to
cook and I want others to know it too!
